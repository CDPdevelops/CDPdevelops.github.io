Be part of the movement

Where was the images captured?

Northern Ireland, North Coast

What camera did you use and what were the settings?

Camera: Cannon E6

Camera Lens: Cannon 24-70 mm

ISO: 250

Aperture: 2.1

Shutter Speed: 1/12000

How long have you been a photographer?

I've been involved with photography for about seven years now.

Was there much preparation in order to capture the photo?

I prepared by. ensuring the camera gear was clean and safe to use in the conditions. I've also never been to this location, so it took me a short period of time to locate the perfect spot to get the right shot captured.

Do you go on these shoots by yourself?

I go 60% of the time by myself, the other 40% i'll have my family or friends out with me.

What area of photography do you enjoy the most?

Deep sea and Aerial photography.

What's the most exciting thing about photography for you?

The stories you can tell on how you captured the photograph, some photographs can speak a thousand words.
