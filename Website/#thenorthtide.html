2019 WINNERS

For the entire year we are running a weekly photo competition based on nature photography. The rules are easy, capture a great picture that relates to nature, follow and tag @northtide on Instagram and post your photo with the hashtag #thenorthtide

During each week four finalists are selected and the public votes used to determine the winner. The winner's image is shared on the @northtide Instagram account.

The winner receives a $100 gift card to northtide.shop and a chance to have his/her photo featured on a Northtide product.

A limited edition clothing collection will be formed based on the best images from the 52 weekly winners. Past winners selected will be contacted in due course regarding details.
